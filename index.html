LAUNCHPAD KERALA 2024 - Newsletter

Introduction

Welcome to the LaunchPad Kerala 2024 Newsletter!

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala, in collaboration with GTech MuLearn, presents an unparalleled opportunity for engineering students and graduates to connect with leading companies in the technical and engineering fields. As one of the most prestigious events organized by IEEE Kerala Section, LaunchPad Kerala 2024 is dedicated to fostering innovation and advancement in Kerala's job market.

Join us as we bring together talented individuals and forward-thinking companies to create meaningful connections and drive progress within Kerala's employment landscape.

Call to Action

Don't miss out on this premier job fair! Whether you're a student seeking internships, a recent graduate exploring career opportunities, or an experienced professional looking for new challenges, LaunchPad Kerala 2024 has something for everyone.

Event Roadmap

  • Participant Registration

    • Start your journey by registering for LaunchPad Kerala 2024. Complete the registration form to create your profile and express your interest in participating in the event.

  • Join the community

    • Join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server and immerse yourself in our vibrant community. Engage with fellow participants, access resources, and participate in challenges to sharpen your skills.

  • Technical Challenge

    • Dive into our unique technical challenge designed to evaluate your technical and non-technical skills. Complete tasks, earn Karma points, and showcase your abilities to top companies.

  • Online Aptitude Test

    • Put your skills to the test in our online aptitude test. Demonstrate your capabilities and compete for opportunities to advance to the next stage of the selection process.

  • Final Interview

    • Shortlisted candidates will have the opportunity to participate in interviews conducted in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode.Receive a confirmation email with your selected interview details. Gear up for the final stage of the selection process and showcase your potential to top recruiters.
